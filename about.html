About US

Our idea Stripped Pizza was founded in 2016 in Zurich with the mission: to provide pizza as a wholesome meal without additives, flavor enhancers and dyes. That's where our name "Stripped" comes from: free of any additives. The recipes are self-created to ensure that each pizza is also balanced and wholesome. That means high levels of fiber, nutrients, proteins and vitamins. We continually develop our dishes so that they are even more wholesome. For example, we enrich our pizzas with vegetables whenever possible. We are also very concerned about quality. The ingredients are always fresh, of high quality and are carefully prepared by hand.

The dough as well as the sauce are prepared daily on site according to our own recipe. Our dough matures for 48 hours so that it is nice and airy and also more digestible. We currently offer four different doughs. Multigrain dough for lovers of "traditional" pizza dough. Whole wheat dough with flaxseed for prudent gourmets. Gluten-free dough for foodies with gluten intolerance. And a new broccoli dough, which consists of one third fresh broccoli, for those who prefer a full-flavored pizza base. Whether vegans, athletes, allergy sufferers or omnivores - it is important to us that everyone receives a wholesome meal with us. Therefore, we offer a variety of different pizzas and of course there is the possibility to compose your pizza according to your own wishes. In addition to our passion for good food, we care about health and well-being in general. So we cooperate with various yoga studios, support football clubs, make ourselves strong for the Cancer League and offer space for creative courses.
